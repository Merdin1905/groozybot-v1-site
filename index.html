Commands and Features

play Lets you play any music you want
stop Closes the currently playing song
skip Move to the next song. If there is no song next, it closes the song
volume Adjusts the sound of the music
replay Automatically replays the same song when the song is finished
pause Pauses the song playing
resume Continue the paused song
istatistik Shows the Bot's Statistics
